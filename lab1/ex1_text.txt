1 Introduction
The topic of this project is the calculation of electron states conﬁned in quantum dots. We consider two-
dimensional (2D) quantum dot with harmonic oscillator potential proﬁle, which works well for the description
of semiconductor quantum dots (due to the high excitation energy in thez direction, eﬀectively electrons are
in the ground state inz, so we need to consider only thex and y dimensions).
The problem comes down to solving the Schrödinger equation
ˆHΨ(⃗ r) =EΨ(⃗ r) (1)
and ﬁnding the eigenenergiesE and eigenfunctions Ψ.
